# Glossary

## Arc

An EVM-compatible Layer 1 blockchain. This project frames Arc as useful for enterprise USDC workflows because USDC can be used for gas.

## USDC

A dollar-denominated stablecoin. In this project, USDC is the asset Ralph deposits and Rita withdraws.

## Owner

The contract administrator. In the demo narrative, Mary is the owner.

## Payer

The wallet authorized to deposit USDC into the contract. In the demo narrative, Ralph is the payer.

## Recipient

The wallet authorized to withdraw available transfers. In the demo narrative, Rita is the recipient.

## Memo

Human-readable context for a transfer.

## Internal reference

An accounting or operations identifier, such as an invoice, payment run, or approval reference.

## Pending transfer

A transfer record funded by the payer but not yet withdrawn by the recipient.

## Untracked USDC

USDC held by the contract that is not assigned to a pending transfer record. This can happen if someone sends USDC directly to the contract address instead of using `deposit`.
